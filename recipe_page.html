Chocolate Pretzel Poke Cake

This delicious, moist chocolate cake is so easy to make! And, it's great for any party during any time of the year!

Ingredients:

  • One boxed chocolate cake (prepared according to the instructions on the box)
  • A stick of butter OR nonstick spray, for greasing
  • 1 can of condensed milk
  • 1 can or jar of caramel
  • 1 container of Cool Whip
  • 2.5 cups of thin pretzels
  • 3 tablespoons of caramel topping

Directions:

  1. Grease a tin foil 9x13 inch tin foil pan using butter or nonstick spray.
  2. Prepare and cook the boxed cake according to the directions on the box.
  3. Let cool 10 minutes, then, using a wooden spoon or chopstick, poke holes throughout the cake.
  4. Pour all of the condensed milk over the entire surface of the cake, making sure it is in all of the holes.
  5. Pour all of the caramel onto the cake, also making sure that it covers all of the surface.
  6. Use a spatula to spread the Cool Whip all over the top of the cake. Make sure it's smooth!
  7. Refrigerate for 4 hours or overnight.
  8. Slice the cake into equal-sized rectangles.
  9. Crush the pretzels into smaller pieces, and sprinkle them over the top of the cake.
  10. Drizzle the 3 tablespoons of caramel on the cake, and ENJOY!

Prep time: 50-60 minutes; Cook time: about 30 minutes, depending on the boxed cake

Best served cold!
